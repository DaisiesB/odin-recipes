Description

A delicious (probably) recipe, lovingly stolen from the internet for the purposes of building this website

Ingrediants

For The Mince

  • 450g minced beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium red pepper
  • as many mushrooms as your heart desires
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbl spoon tomato puree
  • garlic for dayyyzzzz
  • 5 sheets easy cook lasagne sheets
  • salt and pepper to taste

For the White Sauce

  • 25g butter
  • 25g plain flour
  • 425ml milk
  • 50g cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

For the Mince

  1. Preheat the over to 180C
  2. brown the mince
  3. drain off excess fat and add onions, garlic, and tomato puree, cook for 3-4mins
  4. add peppers and mushrooms, cook for 2 min
  5. add chopped tomatoes
  6. bring to the boil and simmer on low heat for 15-20mins

For the White Sauce

  1. Melt butter in saucepan
  2. Add flour and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly
  3. Remove from heat before adding milk, salt and pepper, whisking all the time
  4. Return to the heat, bring to the boil while whisking
  5. reduce to low heat, cook for 3mins, add cheese, cook for 2mins

Final Prep

  1. Put together lasagne
  2. Cook in oven for 30 - 40mins
